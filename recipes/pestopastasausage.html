Pesto Pasta with Sausage

Description

This easy, 20 minute pasta recipe is made with Italian sausage, basil pesto, caramelized onions, and sweet green peas.

Ingredients

  • Bowtie pasta
  • Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Pesto
  • Lemon juice
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Green peas

Steps

  1. Start your Sausage Pesto Pasta by cooking your bow-tie pasta in some salted water until theyâ€™re al dente. Reserve about 1/2 a cup of water to use later, drain, and set aside.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, add the Italian sausage to a hot skillet and break apart. Cook until it is all browned, then remove from pan.
  3. Next, add some olive oil and the sliced onion to the same pan and cook until caramelized, soft, and browned.
  4. Add the cooked sausage back to the pan with the onion, making sure you get any residual juices.
  5. Next add the frozen peas to the pan.
  6. Then add the pesto, reserved pasta water, and lemon juice to create your sauce.
  7. Add the cooked pasta to the pan, season with some salt and pepper, and stir it all together.
  8. Finish by adding the parmigiano-reggiano and freshly cut parsley atop your pasta dish.
  9. Serve and enjoy your Sausage Pesto Pasta with Onions and Peas.
